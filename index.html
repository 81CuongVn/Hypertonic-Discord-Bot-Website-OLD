Welcome to Hypertonic Discord Bot!

Information:

Hypertonic Discord Bot, is a discord bot for everyone to use that is interested in a good Moderation, Social, and Role Management System. Also a good bot that was made for people who want their server to be secure so they can make all their members happy. More custom features will be released soon, if you have any questions, suggestions, or bug reports join support discord by doing !support. If want to know the all the commands for the bot do !cmds, if you want to know the permissions of the commands do !modperms. If you would like to donate do !donate, remember donating is not mandatory but greatly appreciated so quality of the discord bot can increase. Once again thanks for using Hypertonic. Were also a community for developers to work together to create amazing things, also a place for people who want to learn how to code, develop, design, and program! In addition, a place for people to get support and help with our released products!
